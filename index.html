Gaussian pulse and focal spot\(E, I, d_{\mathrm{fwhm}}, t_{\mathrm{fwhm}}, w_0\)
$$\frac{8\ln(2)\,\sqrt{\ln(2)}}{\pi\sqrt{\pi}} = 0.82909\ldots$$
$$E\,(\mathrm{J}) = \frac{I\,(\mathrm{W/cm}^2)}{0.82909}\; t_{\mathrm{fwhm}}\,(\mathrm{fs})\; \Bigl[d_{\mathrm{fwhm}}\,(\mu\mathrm{m})\times10^{-4}\Bigr]^2\times10^{-15}$$
$$I\,(\mathrm{W/cm}^2) = E\,(\mathrm{J})\times0.82909\Big/\Bigl[t_{\mathrm{fwhm}}\,(\mathrm{fs})\;\bigl(d_{\mathrm{fwhm}}\,(\mu\mathrm{m})\times10^{-4}\bigr)^2\times10^{-15}\Bigr]$$
$$t_{\mathrm{fwhm}}\,(\mathrm{fs}) = E\,(\mathrm{J})\times0.82909\Big/\Bigl[I\,(\mathrm{W/cm}^2)\;\bigl(d_{\mathrm{fwhm}}\,(\mu\mathrm{m})\times10^{-4}\bigr)^2\times10^{-15}\Bigr]$$
$$d_{\mathrm{fwhm}}\,(\mu\mathrm{m}) = \sqrt{\frac{E\,(\mathrm{J})\times0.82909}{I\,(\mathrm{W/cm}^2)\;t_{\mathrm{fwhm}}\,(\mathrm{fs})\times10^{-15}}}\times10^4$$
$$w_0\,(\mu\mathrm{m}) = \frac{d_{\mathrm{fwhm}}\,(\mu\mathrm{m})}{\sqrt{2\ln2}},\qquad d_{\mathrm{fwhm}}\,(\mu\mathrm{m}) = w_0\,(\mu\mathrm{m})\sqrt{2\ln2}$$
\(a_0\), intensity, and wavelength\(I, a_0, \lambda\)
$$a_0^2 \simeq 7.2992\times10^{-19}\,[\lambda\,(\mu\mathrm{m})]^2\,I\,(\mathrm{W/cm}^2)$$
$$a_0 = \frac{eE_0}{m_e\omega c} = \frac{e}{m_e c\omega}\sqrt{\frac{2I}{\varepsilon_0 c}}$$
$$I = \frac{a_0^2}{7.2992\times10^{-19}[\lambda\,(\mu\mathrm{m})]^2},\qquad a_0 = \sqrt{7.2992\times10^{-19}[\lambda\,(\mu\mathrm{m})]^2 I}$$
$$\lambda\,(\mu\mathrm{m}) = \sqrt{\frac{a_0^2}{7.2992\times10^{-19} I}}$$
Critical density\(n_c, \lambda\)
$$n_c\,(\mathrm{m}^{-3}) = \frac{\varepsilon_0 m_e}{q_e^2}\Bigl(\frac{2\pi c}{\lambda_{\mathrm{m}}}\Bigr)^2 = \frac{\varepsilon_0 m_e (2\pi c)^2}{q_e^2\lambda_{\mathrm{m}}^2}$$
$$\lambda_{\mathrm{m}} = 2\pi c\sqrt{\frac{\varepsilon_0 m_e}{q_e^2 n_c}}$$
Electron temperature conversion\(T_e\) in eV and K
$$T_e\,(\mathrm{eV}) = \frac{k_B}{q_e}\,T_e\,(\mathrm{K}) \approx 8.617333262\times10^{-5}\,T_e\,(\mathrm{K})$$
$$T_e\,(\mathrm{K}) = \frac{q_e}{k_B}\,T_e\,(\mathrm{eV}) \approx 1.160451812\times10^4\,T_e\,(\mathrm{eV})$$
$$1~\mathrm{eV} = 11604.51812~\mathrm{K},\qquad 1~\mathrm{K} = 8.617333262\times10^{-5}~\mathrm{eV}$$
